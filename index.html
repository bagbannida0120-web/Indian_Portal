India

India, officially the Republic of India, is a South Asian nation with a population exceeding 1.4 billion, making it the world's most populous country. As a federal parliamentary republic with 28 states and 8 union territories, it is also the 7th largest country by area, featuring diverse landscapes from the Himalayas to tropical coasts. India is a rapidly growing major economy, a nuclear-weapon state, and a hub for IT services, with a rich history dating back to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization.Shares land borders with Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Myanmar, and Bangladesh.Varies from tropical monsoon to temperate, with extreme variations ranging from freezing Himalayan glaciers to scorching desert heat.
